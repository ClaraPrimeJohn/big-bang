Welcome to the Big Bang Theory Characters Website

Explore the main characters of the hit TV show, The Big Bang Theory, and learn more about their quirks and personalities.

Sheldon Cooper

Sheldon Lee Cooper is a brilliant yet socially awkward physicist with a passion for science and a love for comic books.

Leonard Hofstadter

Leonard is a physicist who is Sheldon's roommate and best friend. He's known for his intelligence and his on-again-off-again relationship with Penny.

Penny

Penny is the aspiring actress who lives across the hall from Sheldon and Leonard. She brings a dose of normalcy to their geeky world.

Howard Wolowitz

Howard is an aerospace engineer with a penchant for pick-up lines and a unique fashion sense. He is married to Bernadette.

Rajesh Koothrappali

Raj is an astrophysicist who struggles with selective mutism, making it difficult for him to talk to women. He's known for his charm and humor.
